Nostalgic Level Up - Super Mario Bros. World 1-1

I like to participate in Mapcore's monthly challenges whenever I get the chance. In July 2019, the challenge was to recreate a level from a video game. There was a choice of three maps, but only one stuck out to me. World 1-1 from Super Mario Bros. I took the whole month and recreated everything I could using Unity and Blender. I made all the textures, models, animations, programming. Everything from scratch. Except the music, that was done by the talented Jonathan Marlin.
