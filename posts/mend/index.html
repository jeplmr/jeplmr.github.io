Mend - Oculus Launch Pad 2019

Mend is an asymmetric coop game where virtual reality and flatscreen gameplay coalesce. Two players must work together to solve puzzles and explore the world. I built the vertical slice of Mend for Oculus Launch Pad. The project even went on to win the 2019 Launch Pad Scholarship!
