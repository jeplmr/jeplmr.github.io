The Witching Hour - Global Game Jam 2018

The Witching Hour is a horror microgame created in 48 hours at Global Game Jam 2018. The jam theme was Transmission. The player must keep possessed appliances quiet, or suffer the wrath of an evil spirit that haunts the house.
