Ludum Dare 41 - Modern Art Critic Hero

Modern Art Critic Hero is a microgame based on the theme 'Combine Two Incompatible Genres'. Play as The Critic on their personal journey to critique as much modern art as they can. Explore the museum and contemplate life, existence, and the exhibits.

Modern Art Critic Hero won 4th place for Humor in Ludum Dare 41.
